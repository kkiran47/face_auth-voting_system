❓ Login Help

🔐 Forgot Password?

We currently do not support password reset via email. Please contact the system admin to reset your password securely.

📸 Face Not Detected?
  • Ensure your face is well-lit and clearly visible.
  • Do not wear sunglasses or masks.
  • Position your face in the center of the webcam frame.
  • Try recapturing after refreshing the page.
📷 Camera Not Working?
  • Ensure your browser has camera access permissions.
  • Check browser settings (Chrome: lock icon > Site settings > Camera).
  • Make sure no other app is using the webcam.
  • Restart your browser or device if needed.
🧩 Other Issues?

If you're still unable to log in, please contact support or visit your institution's admin desk for manual verification.
